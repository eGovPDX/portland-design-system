# Welcome to the Portland Design System

The Portland Design System is a collection of reusable components, design
tokens, and guidelines built to help teams create consistent, accessible, and
on-brand digital experiences for the City of Portland.

Whether you're a designer, developer, or content author, this system provides
the building blocks you need to build products that feel cohesive and meet the
accessibility standards our residents deserve.
